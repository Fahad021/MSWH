Multiscale Solar Water Heating

What is this project about?

This user interface and the underlying simulation models were created in the scope of a research project. The configurations may break while the user experiments with parameter settings as we are currently able to keep only one copy of the underlying database. The authors are excluded from any liability regarding both the provided functionalities and the obtained simulation results.

The larger scope of the project was to investigate the impact of community vs. individual scale for solar water heating in California.

For more experienced and aspiring beginner Python users it may be of interest to explore the source repository. The repository also provides links to related publications.
